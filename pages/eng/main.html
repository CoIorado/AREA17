AREA 17 is a brand and digital product agency in Paris and New York. We solve problems to serve people. Our work is designed to simplify and engineered to endure.

Founded in 2003, we partner with organizations we trust and admire to create lasting value through craft, impact, and soul.

A novel experience of art and architecture

A unified website for the Fondation Louis Vuitton makes the museum’s exceptional experience more accessible.

Human connection through art and culture

January 2020, Los Angeles, CA

The first phase in redesigning Getty’s digital experience reaffirms an organization’s commitment to sharing it’s art knowledge and resources to inspire deeper curiosity and appreciation of the visual arts.

Advancing our understanding of the brain

July 2020, Geneva, Switzerland

Re-establish and grow the Wyss Center as a groundbreaking and international brand, strengthening its role as a leader in the field of Bio and Neuroengineering.
